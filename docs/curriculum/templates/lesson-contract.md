# Lesson Contract Template

Use this template for new lessons and rewrites that change learner outcomes. Keep tool installation, commands, and cleanup in the lesson README.

```markdown
# Lesson NN: Title

## Goal

State the capability and production relevance in learner language.

## Prerequisites and Entry Check

Name prior lessons, required tools, and a small proof that the learner is ready.

## Mental Model

Explain the concept before commands.

## Guided Local Lab

Give safe local-first steps and expected observations.

## Independent Exercise

State the task, constraints, and evidence the learner must retain. Do not surface solution notes here.

## Failure Scenario

Provide an incomplete, broken, or ambiguous condition. Ask the learner to form and test hypotheses.

## Production Tradeoffs

Describe constraints, alternatives, risks, and decision boundaries.

## Validate and Retain Evidence

Give a validation command or structured review artifact. Explain what a passing result proves and does not prove.

## Clean Up

Remove temporary resources, containers, clusters, files, or cloud resources.

## Recap and Next Step

Recap the capability, ask short verification questions, and name the next lesson or assessment.
```
