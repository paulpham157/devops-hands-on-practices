# Exercise Contract Template

Every independent exercise must state:

1. the competency and expected level;
2. prerequisites and safe local-first setup;
3. a task with constraints rather than only a command to copy;
4. evidence to submit or retain;
5. a verification method and its limits;
6. cleanup;
7. a solution note that is linked only after an attempt in the normal learner flow.

For production-owner exercises, also include an incomplete or ambiguous condition, competing constraints, and a short decision record prompt.
