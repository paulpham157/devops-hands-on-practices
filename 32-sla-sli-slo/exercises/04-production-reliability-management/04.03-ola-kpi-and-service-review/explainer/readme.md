# OLA, KPI, and Service Review

An SLO describes a reliability target, while an OLA describes an internal handoff that helps teams meet that target. A KPI tracks a broader performance or outcome trend. The three should be connected, but they answer different questions.

Use an OLA for ownership, response, escalation, or support expectations between teams. Use a KPI for a trend such as change failure rate, deployment frequency, cost per order, or platform adoption. Do not use a fast response OLA as proof that users received a reliable service.
