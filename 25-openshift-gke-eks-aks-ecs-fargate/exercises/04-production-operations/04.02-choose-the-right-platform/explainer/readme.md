# Choose the Right Platform

The right platform depends on team shape and operational ownership.

Strong signals for OpenShift:

- enterprise platform team
- desire for a curated Kubernetes platform
- stronger built-in platform opinion

Strong signals for GKE, EKS, or AKS:

- Kubernetes is a deliberate standard
- cloud alignment matters
- the team accepts Kubernetes operations

Strong signals for ECS with Fargate:

- AWS-only workload
- no need for Kubernetes API portability
- desire to minimize node management
