# Choose the Right Platform

1. OpenShift. Tradeoff: broader platform surface and OpenShift-specific learning.
2. GKE. Tradeoff: Kubernetes complexity still exists even with a strongly managed experience.
3. ECS with Fargate. Tradeoff: less portability outside AWS and a different contract from Kubernetes.
4. AKS. Tradeoff: still requires Kubernetes operational understanding even with Azure-managed control plane.

The best platform is the one whose operational model the team can actually own.
