# Choose the Right Gate for the Risk

1. It protects forward progress while still raising the quality bar.
2. Repositories differ in language, exposure, maturity, and test coverage.
3. Critical vulnerabilities, serious production-path IaC issues, and failed new-code quality gates.
4. Historical low-risk debt, medium findings with accepted temporary risk, and issues outside the changed path.

The strongest governance is precise enough to be trusted.
