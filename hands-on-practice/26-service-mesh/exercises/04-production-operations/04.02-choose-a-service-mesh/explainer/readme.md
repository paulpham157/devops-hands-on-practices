# Choose a Service Mesh

The right mesh depends on the real problem being solved.

Strong signals for Istio:

- rich traffic management
- strong policy controls
- platform team comfortable with added complexity

Strong signals for Linkerd:

- Kubernetes-first environment
- desire for simpler mesh operations
- need for secure service communication and observability without the broadest policy surface

Strong signals for Consul:

- mixed runtime environment
- broader service networking need outside Kubernetes
